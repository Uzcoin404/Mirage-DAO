Monolog is a peer-to-peer crypto exchange that aims to offer significantly lower fees than other exchanges. In addition to supporting crypto-to-crypto trades, we also offer fiat-to-crypto options, allowing users to buy their favorite coins and tokens using traditional currency.

But what does "peer-to-peer" mean in the context of crypto trading? Essentially, it refers to a system where individuals can buy and sell crypto directly with each other, rather than going through a centralized exchange. For example, if person 1 has 0.5 ETH that they want to sell and person 2 wants to buy 0.5 ETH, person 1 can list their ETH for sale and person 2 can purchase it using a fiat currency. The price will be set at a 1:1 ratio, so you don't have to worry about paying extra.

Overall, Monolog aims to provide a safer and more convenient platform for crypto trading. We believe that our peer-to-peer system, combined with our low fees and support for fiat-to-crypto trades, sets us apart from other exchanges.

One key feature of Monolog is that sellers' listings will be based on the base price of the relevant crypto. So, if the base price of ETH is $1,000, a seller's listed ETH will be worth $1,000 per ETH. However, sellers will not be able to set the price of their listings; instead, listings will be processed on a first-come-first-served basis. This means that the first person to list their ETH will be the first to have it purchased. Another important aspect of Monolog is that user wallets will be kept separate from each other. This means that funds are not pooled together in a single, potentially hackable liquidity pool. Instead, users can import their own wallets and sell the crypto they have acquired or plan to acquire, without having to transfer their funds to a different wallet.

The Goal

Our goal is to establish Monolog as a robust exchange with a large and active user base. To help fund the legal challenges and other obstacles we may encounter along the way, we are launching an NFT (non-fungible token). However, it is unlikely that a functional site will be available prior to the launch of the NFT, as we want to avoid potential legal issues and the associated risks.

If you hold the Monolog NFT in the future, you may be eligible for fee-free buying and selling of your crypto on our platform. This benefit is still being considered, but our goal is to make the NFT as valuable as possible. Additionally, we plan to regularly introduce new features and improvements to Monolog in the future. This is just the beginning of what we believe will be a major project.
