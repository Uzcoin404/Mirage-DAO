GOAL

Our goal is to establish Monolog as a robust exchange with a large and active user base. To help fund the legal challenges and other obstacles we may encounter along the way, we are launching a non-fungible token (NFT). However, it is unlikely that a functional site will be available prior to the launch of the NFT, as we want to avoid potential legal issues and the associated risks.
If you hold the Monolog NFT in the future, you may be eligible for fee-free buying and selling of your crypto on our platform. This benefit is still being considered, but our goal is to make the NFT as valuable as possible. Additionally, we plan to regularly introduce new features and improvements to Monolog in the future. This is just the beginning of what we believe will be a major project.
